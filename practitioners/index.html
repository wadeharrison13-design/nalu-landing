Many practitioners spend too much time trying to find clients

Even highly skilled practitioners often face challenges such as:

  • Relying on social media posts to attract clients
  • Competing in crowded directories where practitioners become listings
  • Inconsistent referrals and unpredictable client flow
  • Spending valuable time on marketing instead of practice
  • Attracting clients who may not fully understand the work or commitment involved

These challenges can make it difficult to focus on what matters most — supporting people.

The Nalu Approach

Nalu Support takes a different approach.

Rather than operating as another directory or advertising platform, Nalu functions as a curated practitioner referral network, focused on thoughtful introductions between individuals seeking support and practitioners whose work aligns with their needs.

During the early phase, practitioners are hand-selected and participation is free while we test and refine matching. Once the network is fully operational, Nalu Support will run on a pay-per-introduction model.
