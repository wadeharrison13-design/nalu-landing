Finding the right practitioner shouldn’t feel so difficult.

Many people searching for support face the same challenges:

  • Scrolling through crowded directories without knowing who is right
  • Asking personal questions in public forums or social media groups
  • Spending hours researching practitioners with little clarity
  • Uncertainty about whether a practitioner truly understands their situation

When you're already looking for support, the process can feel overwhelming.

A more thoughtful way to find support

Nalu Support takes a different approach.

Rather than acting as a directory, Nalu focuses on thoughtful introductions between individuals seeking support and practitioners whose work may align with their needs.

By understanding what you’re looking for, we can help identify practitioners who may be well suited to support you. Our aim is to make the process simpler, more private, and more intentional.

How Nalu Support works

1

Therapy & counselling

Holistic & integrative

Naturopathy / holistic health coaching, Breathwork, Energy healing, Life coaching, Doula support, Mediumship / psychic work

Session investment

Most practitioners in the Nalu network charge between $150–250 per session. During the intake process we ask whether you are comfortable investing within this range. This helps ensure that when introductions are made, both individuals and practitioners are aligned.
